FAQ

Fair questions, honest answers.

Is Placeholder really free? What's the catch?

There isn't one. Conversions run on your device using your browser's built-in capabilities, so we have no server bills to pass on to you. No account, no trial, no watermark, no limits.

Where do my files go?

Nowhere. Files are read directly into your browser's memory, converted there, and offered back as a download. They never leave your machine — you can even disconnect from the internet after the page loads and keep converting.

Is there a file size limit?

No artificial one. The practical ceiling is your device's memory, since everything happens locally. Day-to-day files — photos, songs, spreadsheets — convert effortlessly.

Why isn't my favourite conversion listed?

We only offer conversions that browsers can perform locally and reliably. Formats that require heavyweight or licensed codecs — like encoding full videos — would need server processing, and we'd rather stay free and private than charge you for a render farm.
