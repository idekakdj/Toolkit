What's in the tin

Freehand brushes

Variable-size brush, soft eraser, and a bucket fill — smooth pressure-sensitive strokes right in the browser, no plugins.

Coloring library

A built-in collection of crisp black-and-white outlines. Click any region to flood it with colour — satisfying every single time.

Works with Sigma

Pull your Sigma vector projects into Pigment to colour them in, or send finished paintings to Sigma as a new layer. One Toolkit account connects both.

Saved to your account

Every painting lives in your Toolkit gallery. Start on one device, continue on another — no files to download, no exports to track.
